Overview

This guide is meant to supplement the live tutorial given by Andrew Cumming and Bill Wolf at the Bursting the Bubble workshop hosted at the Lorentz Center at the The University of Leiden . We'll leave this tutorial online for awhile afterwards in the hopes that it proves to be a helpful guide for getting started with bursts in MESA, but these instructions are aimed at participants going through the tutorial at the workshop.

You should complete Part 0 before the tutorial begins on Tuesday afternoon. If you have issues, reach out to Andrew on Monday to get set up in advance of the tutorial.
